so small collection

by youngneil1

A cohesive series of standalone Starfield Creations. Each Creation can be played stand alone, even balancewise. They form a greater whole though - the more of them you combine, the more you see this final shape. The overarching idea is to create a gameworld that feels immensely vast, begs for thorough exploration, is full of mysteries, plays truely challenging and lasts very, very long. In other words the goal of the mods is to make the player feel: so small.

Clicking each of the buttons below will directly lead you to the Creation's official page.

Dynamic Universe

A universe that dynamically scales up with your character level - be it star systems or individual enemies. No more outleveling of content, no more overleveled starts of NG+.

Gear Progression

Adds levels to all weapons, suits, helmets and backpacks. Your character level must match these in order to equip the gear. Levels increase with each NG+.

Economy

Shifts income generation away from loot hauling. Ship building becomes a true long-term goal. Credits are scarce, rewards meaningful.

Explorer

Overhauls resources, crafting, and exploration loop. Rewards are bigger now, but so is the need to explore.

Linearity

New gameplay mode where reloading is your last resort. Live and embrace your adventure as it unfolds, imperfect as it may be.

Slow Travel

Makes journeys more tactile and immersive with grav jump costs, fewer map markers, no overencumbered movement, and limited fast travel.

Star Powers

Rebalances powers for more interesting play without breaking balance — e.g., crowd control, slow time, near invisibility.

The Isotopes

75 new isotopes, each unique to one star system. Required for rank 4 skills and gear mod crafting.

High Level Weapons

Better weapons up to level 300. 7+ new tiers for 50 weapon types, each hidden in a specific star system.

High Level Armors

Armors up to level 255. Mix and match for looks. Starborn armors improve in quality and variety.

Counterfire

From level 85, gain 1% more ground combat damage per level. Works well with High Level Weapons.

Terra Incognita

All surface maps disabled — no markers, no topography, no white dots. Discover the universe without a map.

Helium-4

Grav jumping requires inhaling Helium-4. Lasts 2 jumps, not tradable or storable. Explore moons to find it.

Dusty

Suit Energy depletes quickly on airless worlds. Mine or shelter to survive. Hidden grains of Elder Dust reward the persistent.

Less XP

Removes bonus XP for difficult options, adds controls to lower XP gain and disable crafting XP.

The Waiting Game

Waiting or sleeping sets loot sale profit to zero for 60 minutes. Encourages organic adventuring.

Enter Unity

Origin universe max level is 60, raised with each NG+. Entering Unity underleveled is dangerous.

POI Rotation

New timer and list of discovered POIs prevent repetition. Many hours before encountering the same one twice.

Rarity

Better loot chances when fully surveying star systems. The more you explore, the better the loot.

Health

Health regenerates to a limit. Increase limit by kills; dying or reloading mid-session resets streak.

Journey

Ranks 3 and 4 of skills require multiple Unity crossings and 1000 units of minerals. Embrace the journey.

Waylayer

Random open field encounters between POIs. Adventure between known points.

Time

Only 24 wait/sleep cycles after touching the Artifact — reach next Universe to reset.

Ruin

Alternative to Linearity — embrace permanent consequences in your adventure.

Building Rarity (Achievement Friendly)

Variant of Rarity mod compatible with achievements.

Death

Hardcore permadeath alternative to Linearity.

PPOI Deserted UC Listening Post

An extra handcrafted location for exploration in Starfield.
